This Privacy Policy describes how Learn of Christ ("we", "us", or "the
app") handles information when you use the Learn of Christ website,
iOS app, and Android app (collectively, "the Services").

The short version: Learn of Christ is free to use without an account.
When you use the app without signing in, your annotations stay on your
device. If you choose to sign in, your annotations are backed up to
our servers so you can read across devices. We don't sell your data,
we don't show ads, and we don't use any AI features in the v1 release.

## Information we handle

### When you use the app without signing in

When you read the Bible, open study guides, highlight verses, take
notes, save bookmarks, or track reading progress without signing in,
**all of that data stays on your device.** None of it is uploaded to
us.

To deliver the Bible text and study guides themselves, the app
downloads content packs from our hosting provider (Supabase) the
first time you open a chapter. These downloads happen over standard
HTTPS and our hosting provider's server logs may temporarily record
your IP address as a normal part of operating the service. We do not
attempt to identify individuals from these logs.

### When you sign in

Signing in is always optional. When you choose to sign in with Apple
or Google, we receive:

- A unique account identifier issued by Apple or Google
- An email address (which may be a private relay address that
  forwards to your real address)

We use this only to associate your annotations with you so they can
sync across devices.

When you are signed in, the following data syncs to our servers:

- Your highlights, notes, and bookmarks (which verses, the text of
  any notes you wrote, and the time you created or modified them)
- Your reading progress (which chapter you most recently opened and
  approximately where you were reading in it)
- Your appearance preferences (theme, text size)

We don't combine your annotations with any other data, and we don't
analyze the contents of your notes. They exist for you to read back.

### Account and subscription data

The app currently has no paid features. The schema includes
infrastructure for subscriptions in case we add paid features in the
future; if and when we do, we will update this policy to describe
exactly what billing data is processed and by whom.

### What we do NOT collect

- We do not show advertisements and do not share data with
  advertising networks.
- We do not use AI / chatbot features in the current release. If we
  add them, we will update this policy first.
- We do not collect device identifiers for tracking. We do generate
  a per-install random UUID stored only on your device so the app
  can reconcile your edits across multiple devices when you sign in.
- We do not record analytics events about how you use the app in
  the current release. If we add analytics, we will update this
  policy and provide an opt-out.
- We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or
  location.

## How we share information

We share data only with service providers that are essential to
operating the Services:

- **Supabase** — hosts our database and content storage. They
  process your synced annotations on our behalf under their data
  processing terms.
- **Apple** (for Sign in with Apple) — processes your sign-in. We
  receive only the identifier and email Apple chooses to share.
- **Google** (for Sign in with Google on Android, when available) —
  processes your sign-in similarly.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your
personal information for advertising purposes.

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our
rights, our users, or the public, but we will resist overbroad
requests where lawful to do so.

## Children

Learn of Christ is not directed to children under 13, and we do not
knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you
are a parent or guardian and believe your child has signed in to the
app, contact us at [SUPPORT EMAIL] and we will delete the associated
account and data.

The Bible content itself is available to all readers, including
children, when used without signing in. In that mode no data is
transmitted to us beyond the standard server logs noted above.

## Security

We use HTTPS for all data transmission and rely on Supabase's
production security controls (RLS, server-side encryption, audit
logging) for stored data. No system is perfectly secure; we cannot
guarantee absolute security but we will notify affected users and
relevant authorities of any incident in accordance with applicable
law.
